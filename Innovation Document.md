# 1. Executive Summary

Traditional Third-Party Risk Management (TPRM) platforms operate primarily as compliance repositories. They collect vendor information, store assessments, track certifications, and generate reports.

While useful for governance, these systems remain largely reactive.

SENTINEL introduces a fundamentally different approach.

Instead of functioning as a vendor database, SENTINEL acts as an intelligent vendor risk operating system that continuously understands, predicts, explains, and simulates third-party risk.

The platform combines:

* AI Contract Intelligence
* Hybrid Risk Scoring
* Knowledge Graph Reasoning
* Continuous Vendor Monitoring
* Predictive Risk Forecasting
* Vendor Digital Twin Concepts

to transform vendor management from periodic assessment into continuous intelligence.

---

# 2. Industry Problem

Current Vendor Risk Platforms suffer from five major limitations.

---

## Problem 1

### Static Assessments

Current State:

```text
Quarterly Assessment

↓

Spreadsheet Update

↓

Risk Score Frozen
```

Reality:

Vendor risk changes daily.

Breaches occur unexpectedly.

Certifications expire.

Contracts change.

Financial conditions deteriorate.

Most systems fail to capture these changes in real time.

---

## Problem 2

### Lack of Explainability

Current systems produce scores such as:

```text
Risk Score = 82
```

but cannot answer:

```text
Why 82?

What changed?

What should be done?
```

---

## Problem 3

### Contracts Are Not Intelligence

Most vendor platforms store contracts as files.

They do not understand:

* Data ownership
* Breach notification obligations
* Liability clauses
* Compliance requirements

The most important vendor risk information remains hidden inside PDFs.

---

## Problem 4

### Relationship Blindness

Existing systems evaluate vendors individually.

Reality:

```text
Vendor

↓

System

↓

Data Asset

↓

Business Process

↓

Customer Impact
```

Risk propagates through relationships.

Most platforms cannot model this.

---

## Problem 5

### No Future Awareness

Current tools answer:

```text
What is risky today?
```

Organizations need:

```text
What will become risky tomorrow?
```
